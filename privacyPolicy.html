Privacy Policy

Effective date: November 1, 2023

This Privacy Policy describes how My Website collects, uses, and shares personal information you provide when you access our website or purchase our products and services.

Information We Collect

We only collect information relevant to providing you with our products and services. This includes:

  • Contact information such as email address and phone number
  • Billing information such as credit card number, expiration date, and billing address
  • Information about your transactions and purchases with us

How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Fulfill your product and service orders
  • Send you order and shipping confirmations
  • Process payments and refunds
  • Communicate about orders, products, services, and promotions
  • Analyze how our products and services are used

How Information Is Shared

We do not sell or rent your personal information to third parties. We only share it with third party service providers we use to:

  • Process credit card payments through our payment processor
  • Fulfill orders for products or services through our fulfillment partner
  • Assist with our marketing and promotions through email providers, social media sites, etc

These third parties are restricted from using your personal information in any way other than to provide services for us.

Security

We maintain administrative, technical, and physical safeguards designed to protect the personal information you provide against accidental, unlawful, or unauthorized destruction, loss, alteration, access, disclosure, or use.
